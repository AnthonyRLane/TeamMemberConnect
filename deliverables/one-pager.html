The Problem

When team members need to call in absent, the process relies on phone calls to a front desk that's already managing guests, check-ins, and walk-up requests. Messages get lost, supervisors aren't notified in time, and there's no reliable record of who called when — or why. It's a process built for 1995 running in 2026.
