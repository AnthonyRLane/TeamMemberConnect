The Problem

When team members need to report an absence, they call a front desk that's already juggling guests, check-ins, and walk-up requests. Messages get lost in handoffs. Supervisors aren't notified until it's too late to adjust staffing. And there's no reliable record of who called, when, or why.

It's a process built for 1995 running in 2026.

Team Member Connect replaces the phone-based call-in process with a mobile-first web application that any team member can use from their personal phone — no app download required, no passwords to remember.

Capabilities

📱

SMS Authentication

Team members log in with their employee number and phone. A verification code is sent via text — no passwords to create, reset, or forget.

⚡

Guided Call-In Wizard

Step-by-step flow: select absence type, enter shift details, provide a reason. Submitted and confirmed in under 60 seconds.

🔔

Automated Notifications

Supervisors receive instant alerts when a team member submits a call-in. No relay through the front desk. No missed messages.

📊

History & Tracking

Employees see their own call-in history with real-time acknowledgment status. Managers get department-wide visibility.

📢

HR Announcements

Push targeted updates to employees — open enrollment reminders, schedule changes, policy updates — directly in the app.

🏨

Reception Integration

Front desk staff retain a management view for handling walk-up and phone call-ins, with full search and logging capabilities.

Business Value

Reduce Front Desk Load

Eliminate call-in phone traffic so reception can focus on what matters most — the guest experience.

Real-Time Visibility

Managers know who's out before their shift starts — not after the phone tree finally catches up.

Compliance & Accountability

Timestamped records for every absence. Eliminates "I called in but nobody told you" disputes.

Implementation

01 Configuration & Integration 2–3 weeks

Connect to existing employee directory, configure department routing, set up SMS provider, and customize branding.

02 Pilot Department 2 weeks

Launch with a single department to validate the workflow, gather feedback, and train reception staff on the management view.

03 Full Rollout Ongoing

Expand to all departments with department-specific notification routing and reporting dashboards.
